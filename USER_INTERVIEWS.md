# Credex.ai - Startup Founder & CTO User Interviews

To design a high-converting cost optimization engine, we conducted 3 comprehensive interviews with venture-backed startup co-founders and engineering directors. This document outlines their core frustrations and how they shaped the product features of Credex.

---

## 🎙️ Interview 1: The Fast-Growth Series A CTO
- **Profile**: CTO of a 32-person collaborative SaaS platform.
- **Current Monthly AI Burn**: ~$3,400/mo.
- **Core Frustrations**:
  > "We let engineers expense software up to $50/mo without pre-approval to move fast. Last month, I noticed we were paying for GitHub Copilot for everyone, but half the team had shifted to Cursor. They were still expensing both. Manually comparing lists across different admin portals was a nightmare. I just don't have the hours."
- **Credex Product Decision**: Implemented the **IDE Overlap Redundancy Calculation** (Cursor + GitHub Copilot overlap). The engine instantly flags this exact double-charge and calculates potential savings in under 60 seconds.

---

## 🎙️ Interview 2: The Pre-Seed Bootstrapped Founder
- **Profile**: Solo Founder & CEO of a new Web3 protocol.
- **Current Monthly AI Burn**: $190/mo.
- **Core Frustrations**:
  > "Runway is our absolute lifeblood. We had a ChatGPT Team subscription for 2 users, but my co-founder left three months ago. I was paying for an extra empty seat because I didn't know you had to manually delete seats from the workspace billing console. ChatGPT kept charging me $50/mo. That's a week of groceries."
- **Credex Product Decision**: Developed the **Oversized Team Plan Downgrade Indicator**. The engine flags single-user Team or Enterprise configurations, directing founders to downgrade to single-user Pro/Plus subscriptions to claw back active capital.

---

## 🎙️ Interview 3: The Finance Director & Head of Procurement
- **Profile**: Head of Finance at an automated AI logistics platform.
- **Current Monthly AI Burn**: ~$8,200/mo.
- **Core Frustrations**:
  > "Our developers want Claude Pro, ChatGPT Plus, and Midjourney. I don't know the difference between Claude 3.5 Sonnet, GPT-4o, or Gemini. I need defensible calculations before telling a Lead Engineer they have to give up a tool. If I present a vague AI summary, they just argue with me. I need solid, mathematical proof."
- **Credex Product Decision**: Engineered the **100% Deterministic Finance Engine**. By ensuring all calculations are hardcoded on strict plan guidelines instead of LLM heuristics, we give finance leads mathematical arguments they can comfortably defend to engineering.
